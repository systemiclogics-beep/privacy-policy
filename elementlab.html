Privacy Policy — ElementLab

Last updated: June 20, 2026

ElementLab ("we", "our", "the app") is a periodic table reference and learning app. This Privacy Policy explains what information is collected when you use ElementLab and how it is used.

1. Information We Do NOT Collect

  • ElementLab does not require account creation, login, sign-up, or any personal identification.
  • We do not collect your name, email address, phone number, or location.
  • We do not access your contacts, photos, files, camera, or microphone.

2. Information Collected Automatically

ElementLab uses third-party services that may automatically collect certain data:

  • Advertising (Google AdMob): We use Google AdMob to display banner and interstitial ads to free users. AdMob may collect device identifiers (such as Advertising ID) and usage data to serve relevant ads and measure ad performance.
  • In-App Purchases (Google Play Billing): If you choose to purchase "ElementLab Pro", purchase transaction data is processed securely by Google Play Billing. We do not store your payment information.
  • App Interactions: Basic, non-personal app usage data (such as which features are used) may be collected for analytics and to improve the app experience.

3. How This Data Is Used

  • To display relevant advertisements (for free-tier users)
  • To process and validate in-app purchases
  • To understand app usage and improve features
  • To maintain app functionality and performance

4. Third-Party Services

ElementLab uses the following third-party services, each governed by their own privacy policy:

  • Google AdMob
  • Google Play Billing / Google Play Services

5. Children's Privacy

ElementLab is intended for general audiences, primarily students aged 13 and above. We do not knowingly collect personal information from children under 13.

6. Data Security

All data transmitted by third-party services used in this app (AdMob, Google Play Billing) is encrypted in transit using industry-standard protocols (HTTPS/TLS).

7. Your Choices

  • You can use most features of ElementLab without any data collection beyond standard ad-serving technology.
  • You can opt out of personalized advertising through your device's ad settings (Settings → Google → Ads → Opt out of Ads Personalization).
  • Purchasing "ElementLab Pro" removes ads entirely, which also stops AdMob data collection.

8. Changes to This Policy

We may update this Privacy Policy from time to time. Changes will be posted on this page with an updated revision date.
