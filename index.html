Privacy Policy for TinyABC123

Developer: SystemicLogic

Effective Date: December 2025

1. Introduction

Welcome to TinyABC123, a kids learning application developed by SystemicLogic. This Privacy Policy explains how we handle information when you use the app.

TinyABC123 is designed for children, and we take privacy very seriously. The app does not collect personal information from children.

2. Information We Collect

TinyABC123 does not collect, store, or process any personal data from users.

However, the app uses Google AdMob to display non-personalized banner ads.

AdMob may automatically collect:

  • Device identifiers
  • Advertising ID
  • Approximate location (non-precise)
  • App engagement data (non-personalized)

This data is collected by Google, not by SystemicLogic. All ads shown to children are non-personalized, following Google Play’s Families Policy.

For more details, visit Google’s Ad Policy:
https://policies.google.com/technologies/ads

3. How We Use Information

Since TinyABC123 does not collect personal data, we do not use or store any user information.

AdMob uses limited, non-personalized data to:

  • Serve safe banner ads
  • Prevent fraud
  • Measure ad effectiveness

4. Children's Privacy

TinyABC123 is designed for young children. We comply with Google Play Families Policy Requirements and COPPA. We do not knowingly collect children’s personal information. All ads are set to non-personalized mode.

5. Third-Party Services

The app uses only one third-party service:

Google AdMob – used to display a small banner ad at the bottom of the screen.

6. Data Security

Since TinyABC123 does not collect or store personal information, no user data is stored on our servers.

7. Changes to This Privacy Policy

We may update this Privacy Policy from time to time. Updates will be posted on this page with the new effective date.
